DEAL ADVISORY

M&A, JV & Strategic Transactions

Whether you are acquiring, merging, divesting, or forming a joint venture, Bharat Quantum Prospera provides end-to-end transaction advisory. We manage every phase - from target identification and due diligence through deal structuring, negotiation, regulatory approvals, and post-merger integration.

Process

How we work.

A structured engagement — from first conversation to delivered outcome. Each mandate follows a transparent process so founders always know what's next.

Step 01 / 04

Identify

Screen and shortlist potential targets or acquirers.

Step 02 / 04

Diligence

Comprehensive legal, financial, and tax due diligence.

Step 03 / 04

Structure

Design deal terms for tax efficiency and compliance.

Step 04 / 04

Close

Negotiate, obtain regulatory approvals, and execute.

Scope

What we cover.

The full range of expertise we bring to every engagement in this practice.

  • Buy-side and sell-side transaction advisory - target screening to close
  • Joint venture structuring and strategic partner alignment frameworks
  • Legal, financial, and tax due diligence (domestic and cross-border)
  • Transaction structuring for tax efficiency and regulatory compliance
  • Drafting and negotiation of definitive agreements - SPA, SSPA, SHA
  • Valuation and deal modeling - synergy analysis, control premium, earnout design
  • Virtual data room setup and investor documentation
  • Regulatory approvals - CCI, RBI, SEBI, MCA, IFSCA
  • Post-merger compliance, integration support, and strategic disinvestment advisory
