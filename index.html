About

We don't raise capital. We engineer outcomes.

Bharat Quantum Prospera is where startups meet strategy, and capital meets scalability. We are not another generalist firm — we are a CA-led advisory built for founders breaking ceilings: scaling globally, owning the narrative, and treating compliance as competitive edge rather than a cost of doing business.

From closing multi-million-dollar rounds to taking companies public, we sit behind the work that tomorrow's unicorns will claim as their own. With a sharp focus on IPO advisory, US incorporation, GIFT City & IFSC, global taxation, fund raising, M&A, legal structuring, and international expansion, we ensure founders spend less time worrying about the back office and more time building the future.

Mission. To be the trusted partner to those solving real problems. We simplify complex financial landscapes, unlock capital, and provide expert advisory so founders can focus on the vision while we handle investment banking, legal structuring, and global expansion.

Vision. A world where ambitious founders have unrestricted access to capital, strategy, and global markets — so that breakthrough ideas scale into industries, not pilots.
