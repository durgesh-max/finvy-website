About

A practitioner's mind. A builder's temperament.

I'm CA Durgesh Chavda, founder of Bharat Quantum Prospera — a CA-led advisory firm built for founders who treat compliance as competitive edge rather than a cost of doing business.

I founded BQP to close the gap I kept seeing in my practice: Indian founders raising US venture capital, structuring GIFT City funds, taking SMEs public, and acquiring cross-border targets — all of them needing a partner who understood both the Indian regulatory side (FEMA, RBI, SEBI, ROC, ICDR) and the foreign side (Delaware corporate law, IRC compliance, UK Companies Act, UAE FZE/FZCO frameworks). Most CA firms are great at one. Most boutique investment banks are great at the other. Very few are great at both.

That's where we sit. From the first conversation to the deal closing — capital advisory, M&A, IPO, ESOP, fund structuring, GIFT City, US incorporation, global taxation, international expansion — under one roof, with one accountable partner.
