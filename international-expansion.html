CROSS-BORDER STRUCTURING

International Expansion & Market Entry

Going global is more than registering an entity abroad. Our International Expansion practice covers jurisdiction analysis, regulatory licensing, local tax compliance, banking setup, and end-to-end operational readiness - so you can land in new markets with confidence and start generating revenue faster.

Process

How we work.

A structured engagement — from first conversation to delivered outcome. Each mandate follows a transparent process so founders always know what's next.

Step 01 / 04

Assess

Evaluate target markets, business model fit, and competitive landscape.

Step 02 / 04

Plan

Design entry strategy, entity type, regulatory path, and hiring roadmap.

Step 03 / 04

Register

Incorporate entity, open bank accounts, register for local taxes.

Step 04 / 04

Operate

Set up local accounting, HR, payroll, immigration support.

Scope

What we cover.

The full range of expertise we bring to every engagement in this practice.

  • Jurisdiction analysis and entry strategy - US, UK, UAE, Singapore, Saudi Arabia
  • Cross-border entity setup and structuring (subsidiaries, branches, LLCs)
  • Regulatory licensing - FinTech, Crypto, NBFC, payment aggregators
  • Market feasibility studies and competitive landscape mapping
  • Local tax and compliance frameworks - ZATCA, HMRC, IRS, IRAS, MAS
  • Employment, immigration, and HR compliance (visa, work permits, PAYE)
  • Trade treaties and DTAA optimization for withholding tax reduction
  • End-to-end setup: banking, virtual office, legal, tax, and accounting infrastructure
