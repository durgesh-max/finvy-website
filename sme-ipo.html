MAIN BOARD & SME

IPO Services (Main Board & SME)

Bharat Quantum Prospera provides end-to-end IPO advisory for both Main Board and SME listings - from readiness assessment and financial restructuring through DRHP preparation, SEBI compliance, merchant banker coordination, pricing strategy, roadshows, post-listing governance, and SME-to-Main Board migration.

Process

How we work.

A structured engagement — from first conversation to delivered outcome. Each mandate follows a transparent process so founders always know what's next.

Step 01 / 04

Assess

IPO readiness assessment and gap analysis.

Step 02 / 04

Restructure

Financial restructuring and audit preparedness.

Step 03 / 04

File

DRHP drafting, merchant banker coordination, SEBI filing.

Step 04 / 04

List

Roadshows, pricing strategy, and exchange listing.

Scope

What we cover.

The full range of expertise we bring to every engagement in this practice.

  • IPO readiness assessment and pre-listing gap analysis (Main Board & SME)
  • Financial restructuring, audit preparedness, and Ind AS / GAAP compliance
  • Selection and coordination with merchant bankers, R&T agents, and legal advisors
  • Drafting of DRHP, RHP, prospectus, and all regulatory filings
  • Valuation benchmarking, pricing strategy, and book-building support
  • Statutory compliance - SEBI ICDR regulations, Companies Act, LODR norms
  • Roadshow planning, investor communication, and marketing support
  • Post-listing compliance, quarterly reporting, and investor relations
  • Main Board IPO structuring - book building, offer for sale, fresh issue design
  • SME IPO advisory - BSE SME and NSE EMERGE platform listing
  • SME-to-Main Board migration strategy and execution
  • Anchor investor allocation and institutional placement support
